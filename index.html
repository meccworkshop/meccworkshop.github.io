2nd International Workshop on MetaOS for the Cloud-Edge-IoT Continuum (MECC 2025)

March 31, 2025

Rotterdam

Co-located with the European Conference on Computer Systems (EuroSys)

The 2nd International Workshop on MetaOS for the Cloud-Edge-IoT Continuum (MECC 2025) will be co-hosted at the ACM EuroSys 2025 that will be held from March 30 to April 3, 2025, in Rotterdam, Netherlands. The workshop aims at providing an overview of the opportunities and critical issues brought by a MetaOS orchestrating the Cloud-Edge-IoT ecosystem. Through a series of technical presentations, interactive sessions, and panel discussions, this workshop will address critical issues such as interoperability, standardization, and contribution to relevant open-source projects. Participants will have the opportunity to exchange ideas, share research findings, and collaborate on innovative solutions to advance the field of MetaOS.

Important Dates (Tentative Schedule)

Paper Submission: January 25, 2025
Notification of Acceptance: February 25, 2025
Camera-ready Submission: March 5, 2025
Workshop Date: March 31, 2025

The MECC workshop is a joint initiative from EU MetaOS projects: FLUIDOS, aerOS, ICOS, NebulOuS, NEMO, and NEPHELE.
